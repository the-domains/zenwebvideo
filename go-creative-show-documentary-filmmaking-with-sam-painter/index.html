Go Creative Show: Documentary Filmmaking with Sam Painter

On this week's Go Creative podcast, host Ben Consoli talks to Sam Painter, the cinematographer behind some of the most influential modern documentaries including Going Clear and Steve Jobs: The Man in the Machine. In the podcast they discuss Sam's lighting and shooting techniques, and how he creates beautiful realism in his work.
